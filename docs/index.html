Graduate Algebra I

Instructor: George McNinch

Let \(k\) be algebraically closed and let \(A = k[X_1,\cdots,X_n]\). For an ideal \(I \subseteq A\), \(\mathcal{V}(I) = \emptyset \iff I = A\).
